The AWESOME Games

Paddle Ball

First click on the game display to ensure that the cursor is not active in the code section. Click the restart button at the bottom to begin the game. The paddle will follow your mouse. Use it to keep the ball from getting off the screen! Hit replay to play again and beat your score. Hint: There is a hack to rack up lots of score points! See if you can get lucky and figure it out. (;

Table Tennis

First click on the game display to ensure that the cursor is not active in the code section. Then press the space bar to begin the game. The blue side player presses the "w" key to move up and "s" key to move down while the red side player presses the "o" key to move up and the "k" key to move down. Continue hitting the ball as the score keeps count of how many times the ball goes back and forth until you have a winner! To replay, simply press the space bar.

Block Breaker

Press the green flag to start the game. When you click "Begin" with your mouse, it will set the stage so you can begin playing! Try to break all the enemies without missing the ball. The stage will change each time you press begin so unlimited fun!Go to full screen.

Extinction

To begin game press space bar or click start. Press the up arrow to jump and the down arrow to duck as obstacles come toward you to save the dinosaur. See if you can beat the high score! Go to full screen.
